Hospital Treatment

Inferility OPD

Our Infertility OPD offers comprehensive evaluation and treatment for couples facing infertility. With advanced diagnostic tools and personalized care plans, our specialists aim to provide effective solutions to help you achieve your dream of parenthood.

Eye (B-Scan)

Our Eye (B-Scan) service provides detailed ultrasound imaging of the eye, aiding in the diagnosis of various ocular conditions. This non-invasive procedure is essential for evaluating the eye’s internal structures and detecting abnormalities, ensuring accurate and timely treatment.

X-Ray Reporting

Our X-ray reporting service delivers precise and timely interpretations of X-ray images. Utilizing advanced imaging technology and expert radiologists, we ensure accurate diagnoses to support effective treatment plans for a wide range of medical conditions
